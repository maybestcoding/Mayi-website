I'm May Kwon
Welcome to May-I!

I'm incredibly passionate about learning!

I first came to Canada from South Korea in 2009 to study English. Initially, I planned to stay for only one year, but now I've settled down as a married woman and a permanent resident.
Currently, I work as a team leader at the post office in London Drugs. Previously, I've had various occupations such as physiotherapist, English tutor, newspaper reporter, immigration consultant, and education agent director.
However, my ultimate goal is to become a software developer. Therefore, I'm studying programming very diligently, which brings me excitement and joy.

Playing board games energizes me!

In my free time, I love to play board games. I used to attend a board game meet-up every Friday and sometimes played all night long. Through games, I've made valuable friends and even gained energy.
Currently, I'm joining a board game meet-up every Saturday. Sometimes, we have BBQ board game parties at the beach. Playing games while feeling the beauty of nature is truly wonderful.
My most favorite games are Avalon and Catan. Playing games is like solving problems. When I win, I feel a great sense of achievement.

I never imagined myself as a pet person!

Until I met my first pet Huchu in 2021, I had been indifferent to any animals at all. Sometimes, I wouldn't even remember their existence. However, Huchu completely transformed me into an animal lover. I found myself crying all night long, missing Huchu, after leaving him for just one week at one of my friend's place. He is my first and best pet ever, who taught me how to love these little creatures. Currently, I have two cats named Cherry and Sangchu. Watching them play together brings me immense joy.
It's also very interesting to know that every animal has a different personality, just like human beings do.
